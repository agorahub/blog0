昨天在「湾区文化沙龙」做了一场题为「司法种族主义，警察暴力与抗议中的暴力」的讲座，全程视频回放见：https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=hgV5czJA3JU。

这是「美国种族问题线上沙龙」系列的第一期，接下来几周由游天龙、唐韵、北大飞、夕岸等几位朋友分别主讲，感兴趣的朋友可以在此报名。

应讲座听众要求，以下推荐一些延伸阅读材料（以书为主；论文太多，只能略举一二）：


一、关于美国法律如何从城市设计、住房贷款、社会福利资格等各个方面歧视黑人，最终将黑人聚集区打造成“超级贫困区”

Michelle Wilde Anderson (2010), “Mapped Out of Local Democracy”

Ira Katznelson (2005), When Affirmative Action Was White: An Untold History of Racial Inequality in Twentieth-Century America

Richard Rothstein (2017), The Color of Law: A Forgotten History of How Our Government Segregated America

-         我在《种族隔离阴霾下的罗斯福新政：被挟持的宪政转型及其后果》中介绍过Katznelson和Rothstein这两本书里的一部分内容

Jessica Trounstine (2018), Segregation by Design: Local Politics and Inequality in American Cities


二、关于“超级贫困黑人区”与“大规模囚禁”之间的恶性互动

Loïc Wacquant (2004), Deadly Symbiosis: Race and the Rise of Neoliberal Penality

-         这本书的“太长不看”版：Loïc Wacquant (2001), “Deadly Symbiosis: When Ghetto and Prison Meet and Mesh”


三、关于“大规模囚禁”的前因与后果

Michelle Alexander (2010), The New Jim Crow: Mass Incarceration in the Age of Colorblindness

-         几年前我们在「选·美」播客里聊过这本书：https://xuanmei.us/10

Bruce Western & Becky Pettit (2010), “Incarceration & Social Inequality”

Khalil Gibran Muhammad (2011), The Condemnation of Blackness: Race, Crime, and the Making of Modern Urban America

Elizabeth Hinton (2016), From the War on Poverty to the War on Crime: The Making of Mass Incarceration in America

James Forman Jr. (2017), Locking Up Our Own: Crime and Punishment in Black America

-         我在《荐书三本｜美国种族问题类，深入浅出型》中推荐过这本书

Christopher Uggen & Jeff Manza (2006), Locked Out: Felon Disenfranchisement and American Democracy

Elizabeth Hull (2006), The Disenfranchisement of Ex-Felons


四、关于美国警察的历史、阶级性与种族性

Sam Mitrani (2013), The Rise of the Chicago Police Department: Class and Conflict, 1850-1894

-         这本书的“太长不看”版：Alex Gourevitch (2015), “Police Work: The Centrality of Labor Repression in American Political History”

Joe Soss & Vesla Weaver (2017), “Police Are Our Government: Politics, Political Science, and the Policing of Race–Class Subjugated Communities”

Michael White & Henry Fradella (2016), Stop and Frisk: The Use and Abuse of a Controversial Policing Tactic


五、关于“黑人保守派”（以及所谓的“伯克利教授匿名信”）

Leah Wright Rigueur (2014), The Loneliness of the Black Republican: Pragmatic Politics and the Pursuit of Power

Corey Robin (2019), The Enigma of Clarence Thomas


六、关于中文舆论界的“文革想象”与“灯塔主义”

《特朗普、共和党与美国当代右翼极端主义》

《问答两则：移除罗伯特·李的雕像对美国是利是弊？要移除李雕像，就得一并移除华盛顿雕像吗？》

《「我也是」：作为集体行动的公共舆论运动》第三节《「舆论审判」与文革创伤后应激》

-         全文下载：https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3463859

《灯塔主义与中国自由派知识分子的「川化」》

-         全文下载：

o  正式发表版（需要学校订阅该期刊）：https://www.tandfonline.com/doi/abs/10.1080/10670564.2020.1766911?journalCode=cjcc20

o  非正式版（不需订阅即可免费下载，但文字与正式发表版略有出入）：https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3538736
