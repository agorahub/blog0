揚，

1986年諾貝爾和平獎得獎者 Elie Wiesel 得獎發言:「當世上何時何地有人類受苦受辱，一定要選邊站。保持中立只會助長壓迫者，而不是受害者。 」Elie 本身就是納粹猶太大屠殺的倖存者，今天我們讀他的【Night】。

本書取材自 Elie 的自身經歷，記載他和父親1944年至1945年間，二次大戰接近尾聲、猶太人大屠殺的極端時期，被送往納粹德國奧斯威辛等集中營的種種際遇。集中營的經歷令他認為神已死並對人性厭惡。

Never shall I forget that night, the first night in camp, that turned my life into one long night seven times sealed.

Never shall I forget that smoke.

Never shall I forget the small faces of the children whose bodies I saw transformed into smoke under a silent sky.

Never shall I forget those flames that consumed my faith for ever.

Never shall I forget the nocturnal silence that deprived me for all externality of the deslre to llve.

Never shall I forget those moments that murdered my God and my soul and turned my dreams to ashes.

Never shall I forget those things, even were I condemned to live as long as God Himself.

Never.

當年 Elie 所住是一個偏遠小村莊，村民原先以為集中營大屠殺只是中古時代的歷史，不會發生在二十世紀。諷刺的是，集中營又發生在廿一世紀的新疆。如果 Elie 在世，必定勇於發聲。
